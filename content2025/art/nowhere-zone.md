the "NOW*HERE zONE" is an interactive art exchange treasure box nestled in the woods inviting participants to give and receive materials of intrigue in the form of art, words, objects—blurring the boundaries of time. Each exchange links past creators with future recipients, forming a collective present. Please accept our gifts as a reminder of the NOW and our interconnectedness. Be here now and find the present in the presence. This evolving space is a meditation on presence, impermanence, and the interconnected nature of time. It reminds us that everything that has ever been and will ever be already exists—right here, in this moment. In the act of exchange, we step into the eternal now—into the nowhere, which is everywhere: NOW HERE
