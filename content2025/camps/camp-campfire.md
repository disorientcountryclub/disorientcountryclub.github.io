Gather 'round and get toasty at Camp Campfire — where the flames are friendly, the vibes are nostalgic, and the s'mores are next-level. We're serving up both sweet AND savory s'mores, while inviting you to share your best campfire stories — from spooky tales to hilarious mishaps to heartfelt moments under the stars.

Scout badges may or may not be involved

Friday & Saturday nights, 8:30 PM - 10:30 PM. Maybe even later – scouts honor!
