title: "The Boogie Down Lounge"
---

Introducing the Boogie Down Lounge a Bedouin-inspired chill experience.

We've all been there. Feeling overwhelmed, needing a place to re-center, a place to take a breath. We welcome you to our space to have some cucumber water or a chai while listening to soothing melodic international-inspired music at the BOOGIE DOWN LOUNGE.

Under a tent in the woods, find comfort with soft pillows, comfy blankets, chill aromas, and soft lighting. Find your inner peace or just take a respite before getting back to the party. We are your hosts and welcome you to join us in this adult space.

So welcome to the BOOGIE DOWN LOUNGE, we ask you kindly remove your shoes and be mindful of the other guests. As this is a chill spot we also ask that you keep it to a whisper. Help yourself to the refreshments available. Please take with you what you bring in.
