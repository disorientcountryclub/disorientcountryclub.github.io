## How Do I Get There?

Our event takes place near Shandaken, NY, 2 ½ hours north of NYC. We will release the exact location the week of the event.

**Transportation Options:**

- **DRIVING**: 2 ½ hours north of NYC. Exact location released week of 5/21.
- **FLYING**: We recommend flying into Newark-EWR and renting a car from the airport. For this option expect 2 ½ hours of drivetime up. Alternatively, fly into Albany-ALB with a 1 ½ hour drive to gate.
- **UBER/LYFT/ETC**: People have done it, prices vary
- **RIDESHARE FROM NYC**: Check the [Rideshare board](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-loCO326kkTCRD_dok8ZOR5EhRYji_IZDiRfa69sHHY/edit?gid=0#gid=0) and the FB event page
- **BUS**: Take the Trailways bus to Phoenicia and arrange a friend onsite to scoop you up. Phoenicia Trailways Bus stop is 10 mins away from our gate. Tell the driver your stop is Shandaken. [https://webstore.trailways.com/](https://webstore.trailways.com/)
- **TRAIN**: AMTRAK to Rhinecliff Stop ID: RHIN. Closest stop is 50 minutes away from our gate. You must arrange a ride from Rhinecliff.
- **TOWN CAR SERVICE**: Woodstock Town Car [woodstocktowncar.com](http://woodstocktowncar.com) (845) 679-6656 Cars, Limos Passenger Vans, service from NYC and Surrounding areas directly to our gate.
- **SMALL PLANES/HELICOPTER**: Hunter Mountain Airport, ICAO: 97NY, 26 minutes from gate
