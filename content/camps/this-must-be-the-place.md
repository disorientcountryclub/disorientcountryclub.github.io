A portal disguised as a camp, nestled between the ordinary and the magical. We’re a crew of dreamers, dancers, builders, and late-night philosophers who’ve come together to create a cozy, vibrant haven at the Country Club. Our camp is about grounding in the now—through connection, joy, and just the right amount of weird.
By day, find shade, chill beats, massage stations and restorative elixirs at our shady chill space, where strangers become family. When night falls… well, that’s when the magic really starts. Expect spontaneous dance parties, storytelling under the stars, and interactive art installations that ask the big questions (and answer them with glitter).
We’re fully fueled by love, intention, and sensation. Whether you're stopping by for a moment or moving in for a while—if you feel it, then yes... This Must Be The Place.
