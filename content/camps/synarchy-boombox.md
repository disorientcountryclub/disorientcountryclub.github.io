 A Giant… BOOMBOX?! (Yes. Obviously.)  Behold the SYNARCHY BOOMBOX— a gloriously oversized relic of rhythm, scaled up to 16 feet wide, 8 feet tall, and fully committed to the bit.  This is not a sculpture pretending to be a boombox. This is a boombox that ate a sculpture and kept growing.  Step inside and you’ll find yourself inside the music; sound ricocheting through the chamber, bass wrapping around your ribs, light dancing like it knows something you don’t. It’s part instrument, part environment, part friendly sonic creature.  Step outside and she is projecting waves of sound and color reminding you that you are a star.   Now… let’s make it misbehave   With big, beautiful, irresistible, functional….. knobs.  No instructions. No right answers. Just curiosity and consequence.  This is not a passive listening experience. This is a playable moment.  A place where strangers become collaborators.  where sound becomes something you can touch.  and where a giant boombox invites you to ask:  What happens if I turn this?  Go ahead. It’s begging for it.
