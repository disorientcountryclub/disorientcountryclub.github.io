The "BOOGIE DOWN LOUNGE" is back!! 

We've all been there. Feeling overwhelmed, needing a place to re-center, a place to take a breath from the festivities. WE welcome you to take that breath at the Boogie Down Lounge. Enjoy some cucumber water or some hot chai while listening to soothing melodic international inspired music. We are a bedouin-inspired chill space experience. Entering through the draped portal, you will be transported into a calming retreat, a transcendental realm that reconnects mind body and spirit.

Snuggled in the woods, find comfort with so soft pillows, cozy blankets, wafting aromas and soft lights. Find your inner zen or just a brief respite before jumping back into the party. As your hosts, we welcome you to join us. 

We kindly ask you to remove your shows before entering, be mindful of other guests and whisper, leave no trace and adults only please.
