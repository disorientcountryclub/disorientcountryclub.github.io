The mission of Disorient is to make people happy. As we get bigger and more organized, it's important to remember that what makes Disorient special is that by joining together, we can bring happiness to more and more people. That is what we do on the playa. It is what we strive to do off the playa. Volunteer to join us. It is our foundation. It is our heart. Join the volunteer squad. Help us make the magic that is the Country Club experience. It is not a great way to meet new people and get involved, it is the BEST way. You will truly feel a part of the fold once you have been a part of the creation of a Disorient event.

&nbsp;

Want to volunteer for Country Club? Unsure of what you can do? Do not fret, my friend, no specialized skills are necessary. We will find a place for you, we promise.

## Volunteering

Our community considers volunteering to be a Gift to the community, an example of Communal Effort, and a show of Civic Responsibility — three of the 10 Burning Man Principles. It also opens the door to new friendships that carry on throughout the event and into the default world, and gives us a chance to feel a sense of pride when we look around and say "I helped to make that possible!"

We can't wait to see you!!!!!!

&nbsp;

### Lead Roles

{{< role-gallery >}}

{{< role name="Alpha Lead" icon="hammer" type="lead" >}}
Onsite set-up lead, coordinating Alpha Volunteers. Makes sure stages are built, portos are placed, tents, generators, and other equipment arrives and goes into the correct places. Oversees building projects such as Zero G chairs.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Art Director" icon="palette" type="lead" >}}
Designs stages, chill spaces, and other common areas. May designate spaces to be designed/decorated by others. Manages art placement with artists who bring pieces to display throughout the event. Manages the art budget.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Gate Lead" icon="gate" type="lead" >}}
Train gate volunteers, ensure they show up for their shift and record their hours, cover shifts for no-shows. May involve ad hoc recruiting of more volunteers during the event. Works closely with Parking Lead.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Parking Lead" icon="car" type="lead" >}}
Train parking volunteers, ensure they show up for their shift and record their hours, cover shifts for no-shows. Works closely with Gate Lead.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Medical Lead" icon="medical" type="lead" >}}
For trained medical professionals (doctors, nurses, EMS, first aid responders). Ensure available first aid supplies are sufficient, train/brief medical volunteers, ensure they show up for shifts. Develops a plan for any medical issues/emergencies, and is always prepared to call outside the venue for help if needed.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="LNT Lead" icon="leaf" type="lead" >}}
Responsible for ensuring we are good stewards of the land and return the property in as good as, if not better, condition than we arrived. Creates clearly marked receptacles for garbage and recycling, keeps an eye on habits of event goers, and liaisons with Disengage Lead post-event.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Fire Lead" icon="flame" type="lead" >}}
Marks the perimeter of the burn field to ensure safety. Guides fire volunteers in their perimeter roles during the Temple Burn. Ensures any accelerant is used safely. Guardian of the fire until flames have died out and the coal/ash pile is safe to leave unattended.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Conclave Lead" icon="fire-spin" type="lead" >}}
For experienced fire performers. Organize those interested in spinning fire during conclave, review basic fire safety protocol, ensure fire performers are wearing fire resistant outfits. Ensure fuel dump is in a safe location.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="DPW Lead" icon="wrench" type="lead" >}}
Responsible for set-up of basic event infrastructure such as gate/medical tent. Partners with City Planning for theme camp placement and road signage. Assists Production with infrastructure needs. Ensures wooded roads are safe, including coordinating temporary road maintenance as needed.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Disengage Lead" icon="box" type="lead" >}}
Ensures that the land is returned to how it was before the event began. Stays on site until all infrastructure and equipment is either picked up by rental companies or returned to the on-site storage container. Coordinates with LNT Lead for MOOP sweeps and ensures the production house is clean.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Lady of the Manor" icon="compass" type="lead" >}}
A multi-day role for a seasoned Country Club camper who is very familiar with the property. Assists campers to find suitable camping spots away from installations and infrastructure. Carries a radio and is contacted by Gate when a camper needs help finding a spot.
{{< /role >}}

### Volunteer Roles

{{< role-gallery >}}

{{< role name="Alpha" icon="hammer" type="volunteer" >}}
Ad hoc building (erecting tents, building Zero G chairs, light woodworking), laying down tarps, hanging items, moving equipment, decorating, and getting materials from the on-site storage container.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Art" icon="palette" type="volunteer" >}}
Alpha volunteers who assist the decoration of the event including setting up fabric, stages, signage, LED installations, and decorating common areas. May also assist artists setting up their work.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Gate" icon="gate" type="volunteer" >}}
Greet new arrivals, check photo identification against the guest list, explain event details, and direct them to drop off gear and park. Ad hoc recruiting of open volunteer shifts.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Parking" icon="car" type="volunteer" >}}
Ensure cars park appropriately in the lot, close to each other to maximize space and in spots that are safe for the type of vehicle.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Medical" icon="medical" type="volunteer" >}}
For trained medical professionals. On call, carrying a radio for a shift during the event and responding to emergencies as they arise.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Stage" icon="music" type="volunteer" >}}
Best for those with DJ/sound experience. Greet DJs before their sets, answer questions about equipment, and liaise with Sound Lead when escalations arise. Carries a radio while on shift.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="LNT" icon="broom" type="volunteer" >}}
Work with the LNT Lead conducting MOOP sweeps, gathering trash and recycling for removal from the property.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Fire" icon="shield" type="volunteer" >}}
Stand perimeter for Temple Burn to ensure attendees do not cross the perimeter. Move attendees further back as needed.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Disengage" icon="box" type="volunteer" >}}
Removal of all temporary structures, decorations, lighting, and moving materials back to the storage container. Cleans production house and conducts MOOP sweeps.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Gas & Supply" icon="fuel" type="volunteer" >}}
Drive your own vehicle off-site to pick up gas for generators each day. Check in at radio station at noon each day.
{{< /role >}}

{{< role name="Truck Driver" icon="truck" type="volunteer" >}}
Pick up rental truck, drive to Brooklyn warehouse to load, then drive to the event site. After the event, drive the rental truck back to Brooklyn warehouse to unload, then return the truck.
{{< /role >}}
